About

Welcome to Thames Valley Perl Mongers! This group exists for anyone in the region of the Thames Valley, UK to have fun learning about the Perl computer programming language.

It doesn't matter how much experience you have with Perl: the group exists for everyone from complete beginners to experienced developers.

Meetings

We meet once a month for drink and good conversation in several venues across Marlow, Reading, Didcot, Oxford, and other places in the Thames Valley..

Occasionally we also have technical talks on a variety of topics, delivered by our members or guests.

Getting In Touch

Please join our mailing list and introduce yourself to others. You can read the list archives online.

If you're visiting the area and would like to meet up with us or arrange an impromptu meeting for any occasion, please join the list and let us know!
